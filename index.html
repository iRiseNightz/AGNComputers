About Aesir Gaming Network

Who are AGN?

agn.com is a major supplier of PC compatible computer components and peripherals across Ireland. We supply thousands of end users, students, public and private sector companies, central government offices and education providers each year with quality products and services, all at highly competitive prices.

The IT business was established in Dublin in 1993 by our current managing director, Aria Tahi. Aria was awarded an engineering degree with honours in Electronics from the University College Dublin in 1991; and then worked for a period in the electronics industry before attending the Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT), where he gained his postgraduate MSc.

Based upon his experience in the electronics and computer world, Aria established Aspire Technology with the aim of making computer products and services more accessible for individuals and businesses by ensuring quality products were sourced and distributed to the marketplace at affordable prices.
